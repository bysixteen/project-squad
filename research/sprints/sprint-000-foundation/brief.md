## Sprint Challenge

Before building any feature, the squad needs shared context. What is Fieldside? Who are the users? What constraints are we working within? What decisions have already been made, and what are we not building? This sprint establishes the foundation that every subsequent sprint will build on.

**Long-term goal:** A grassroots football club secretary can manage their entire club — fixtures, squad availability, payments — from one app, spending less than 10 minutes per week on admin.

**Sprint question:** What shared understanding does the squad need before Sprint 001, and what constraints will govern every subsequent decision?

---

## Sprint Questions

1. Who are the users, and what do they actually need?
2. What are the hard technical constraints for v1 (solo developer, zero budget, GDPR)?
3. What design principles should govern every screen we build?
4. What tech stack decisions have already been made, and what is still open?

---

## Target User

Sarah (club secretary) — the primary user and adoption gatekeeper. If the app doesn't make her life easier, it will not be adopted.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- `research/PERSONAS.md` contains at least two detailed personas with goals, pain points, and key quotes
- `research/PRINCIPLES.md` contains at least three actionable, constraint-based principles (not aspirations)
- `research/DECISIONS.md` contains the tech stack and auth decisions with rejected alternatives
- `research/sprint-backlog.md` is seeded with at least five sprint candidates
- All personas understand the secretary-first constraint
