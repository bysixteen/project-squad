⚠ The Trap

Designing everything at once

Large projects fail when teams try to solve the whole problem in one go. Context gets lost. Decisions conflict. The "Big Bang" approach creates noise, not clarity.

✓ The Solution

A sequence of focused sprints

You don't need a bigger sprint. You need a sequence of small ones. Each sprint targets a single moment in the user journey. One moment. One decision. Done.
